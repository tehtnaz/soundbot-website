soundbot

A music bot with an extensive feature list, all those essential for you and your servers

Invite (opens new window)

All Major Platforms

soundbot can play any song or playlist from YouTube, SoundCloud, Spotify, or Bandcamp and even any audio file/attachment you give it

Custom Playlists

Playlists you can create, edit, and even share with friends using colloborative playlists! Combine songs from any supported platform!

Moderation Settings

Allows you to set a DJ role and limit the usage of some (or ALL) commands to only DJs
