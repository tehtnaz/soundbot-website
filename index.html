# soundbot

A music bot with moderation features, support for all the most popular platforms, and extensive playlist creation system

# About

soundbot plays any song/playlist from YouTube/SoundCloud/Spotify/Bandcamp and even any audio file/attachment you give it! You can also save your own playlists to your account for personal use later. It's also possible to share playlists with others and let them edit it!

Support Server: https://discord.gg/kC9WPJFREf
